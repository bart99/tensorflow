Shapes
 

Insert > Illustrations > Shapes

In Hanword documents, you can insert and edit straight lines, rectangles, ellipses, arcs, polygons, curves, freehand lines, object connectors, and text.

More Information

Straight Line

To draw a straight line on the edit screen.

Rectangle

To draw a rectangle on the edit screen.

Ellipse

To draw an ellipse on the edit screen.

Arc

To draw an arc or a fan on the edit screen.

Polygon

To draw a polygon made of straight lines like a triangle or draw any type of polygon by dragging the mouse.

Curve

To draw a curve on the edit screen.

Freehand Line

To draw a picture freely on the edit screen, as if you were writing or signing on paper with a pen.

Object Connector

You can draw straight lines or freehand lines and connect two drawing objects on an organization chart or flowchart by using Object Connector. If you connect drawing objects and move one of them to another position, the object connector moves as well.

Text Box

You can use the Text Box to insert a long title into a multiple column document, and also to insert a boxed summary in the middle of the body. You can easily adjust a text box by changing its position and size, fill effect for the inside, and also modify the shape and border color.
