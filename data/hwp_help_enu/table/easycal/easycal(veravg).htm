Vertical Average
 

Table > Quick Formula > Vertical Average

It calculates the vertical average from the topmost cell to the cell immediately above the cell at the cursor position and automatically enters the result in the current cell.

Steps

  1. In a table like the one shown below, place the cursor at the cell where you want the average score for the Korean subject to appear.

  2. Click Table > Quick Formula > Vertical Average.
  3. 81.67 is entered into the average score line automatically. The average is calculated to two decimal places.

Note

Recalculating Formula

If you modify the cell content you used in Quick Formula, the result gets applied automatically. If you modify the result, however, recalculation does not occur until you modify the cell content you used in Quick Formula or Modify the formula.

Ignoring Previous Calculation Result

If a Formula result is contained in the cell directly preceding the vertical average result cell, which in other words is the last cell used to calculate Vertical Average, this value will not be included in the vertical average calculation.
