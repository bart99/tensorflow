Vertical Sum
 

Table > Quick Formula > Vertical Sum

It calculates the vertical sum from the topmost cell to the cell immediately above the cell at the cursor position and automatically enters the result in the current cell.

Steps

  1. In a table like the one shown below, place the cursor on the second cell on the fifth row. This cell is where the total score for the Korean subject will be entered.

  2. Click Table > Quick Formula > Vertical Sum.
  3. 245 is entered into the total score for the Korean subject line automatically. This total is gained by calculating the values up to the cell immediately above the cell at the cursor location.

Note

Recalculating Formula

If you modify the cell content you used in Quick Formula, the result gets applied automatically. If you modify the result, however, recalculation does not occur until you modify the cell content you used in Quick Formula or Modify the formula.
