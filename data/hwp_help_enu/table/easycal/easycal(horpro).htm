Horizontal Product
 

Table > Quick Formula > Horizontal Product

It calculates the horizontal product from the leftmost cell to the cell immediately preceding the cell at the cursor position and automatically enters the result in the current cell.

Steps

  1. In a table like the one shown below, place the cursor on the fourth cell on the second row. This cell is where the total price of cucumbers will be entered.

  2. Click Table > Quick Formula > Horizontal Product.
  3. 1250 is entered into the cucumber total line automatically. This total is gained by calculating the values up to the cell immediately preceding the cell at the cursor location.

Note

Recalculating Formula

If you modify the cell content you used in Quick Formula, the result gets applied automatically. If you modify the result, however, recalculation does not occur until you modify the cell content you used in Quick Formula or Modify the formula.
