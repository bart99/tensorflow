Horizontal Average
 

Table > Quick Formula > Horizontal Average

It calculates the horizontal average from the leftmost cell to the cell immediately preceding the cell at the cursor position and automatically enters the result in the current cell.

Steps

  1. In the following table, where the total score for Hong GilDong was gained through Table > Quick Formula > Horizontal Sum, place the cursor in the cell where you want the average score of Hong GilDong to appear.

  2. Click Table > Quick Formula > Horizontal Average.
  3. 80.00 is entered into the average score line automatically. The average is calculated to two decimal places.

Note

Recalculating Formula

If you modify the cell content you used in Quick Formula, the result gets applied automatically. If you modify the result, however, recalculation does not occur until you modify the cell content you used in Quick Formula or Modify the formula.

Ignoring Previous Calculation Result

If a Formula result is contained in the cell directly preceding the horizontal average result cell, which in other words is the last cell used to calculate Horizontal Average, this value will not be included in the horizontal average calculation.
