Horizontal Scroll Bar
 

View > Document Window > Horizontal Scroll Bar

You can move the scroll bars up/down or left/right to view parts of a document that are not visible on the current screen.

You can hide the horizontal scroll bar if necessary and enlarge the edit screen by the height of the horizontal scroll bar.

Steps

To view/hide the horizontal scroll bar

  1. Click View > Document Window > Horizontal Scroll Bar.
  2. The selection mark in front of the Horizontal Scroll Bar option is removed to indicate that the option has been unselected.

    You can then verify that the document window has been lengthened by the height of the vertical scroll bar.

  3. Click View > Document Window > Horizontal Scroll Bar again.
  4. The selection mark is displayed again in front of the Horizontal Scroll Bar option and so does the horizontal scroll bar on the edit screen.
