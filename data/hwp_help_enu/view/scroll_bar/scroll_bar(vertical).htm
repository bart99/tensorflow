Vertical Scroll Bar
 

View > Document Window > Vertical Scroll Bar

You can move the scroll bars up/down or left/right to view parts of a document that are not visible on the current screen.

With the vertical scroll bar, you can choose to have it displayed on the screen or hide it to free up additional space on the edit screen.

Steps

To view/hide the vertical scroll bar

  1. Click View > Document Window > Vertical Scroll Bar.
  2. The selection mark in front of the Vertical Scroll Bar option is removed to indicate that the option has been unselected.

    You can then verify that the document window has been widened by the width of the vertical scroll bar.

  3. Click View > Document Window > Vertical Scroll Bar again.
  4. The selection mark is displayed again in front of the Vertical Scroll Bar option and so is the vertical scroll bar on the edit screen.
