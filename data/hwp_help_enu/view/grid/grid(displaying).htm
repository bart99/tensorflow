Show Gridlines
 

View > Gridlines > Show Gridlines

With gridlines enabled, a grid of dots or lines having fixed vertical and horizontal spacing is displayed on the edit screen. Gridlines provide precision positioning control when drawing or editing a picture.

Steps

To display or hide gridlines

  1. Click View > Gridlines > Show Gridlines.
  2. The selection mark is displayed in front of the Show Gridlines option, and gridlines are inserted into the document window.
  3. Click View > Gridlines > Show Gridlines again.
  4. The selection mark in front of the Show Gridlines option is removed, along with the gridlines from the document window.

Note

Gridlines and Printing

Gridlines are displayed in the document window when the Show Gridlines option is selected. However, gridlines are guidelines for drawing or editing pictures on the edit screen only and are not displayed on document printouts.

Types of Gridlines

Available gridline types are Dot, which inserts dots at fixed horizontal and vertical intervals, and Line Grid, which connects grid points with lines. Select the desired gridline type from Show Gridline under View > Gridlines > Gridline Settings.

Shortcut to Gridline Settings

The View Preferences icon is located at the base of the vertical scroll bar on the right side of the Hwp edit screen.

Clicking this icon opens a collection of icons. From this collection, click on the ViewGridOption icon to open the Gridline Settings dialog box and change the gridline settings.
