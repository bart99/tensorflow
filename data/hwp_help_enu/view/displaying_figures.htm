Image
 

View > Show/Hide > Image

Generally, images inserted into the body in Hwp are shown on the edit window. This is when Hwp is set to show images. If Hwp is set to hide images, inserted images are not shown and only their positions are indicated as to allow faster editing.

Steps

To show/hide image

  1. Click View > Show/Hide > Image.
  2. The selection mark in front of the Image option is removed. The images are also removed from the document and replaced with insertion indicators.
  3. Click View > Show/Hide > Image again.
  4. The selection icon in front of the Image option is restored, and images are again shown in the document.

Note

Toggling Show/Hide Image

View > Show/Hide > Image toggles between show image and hide image settings. A selection mark in front of Image indicates that images are shown. Without the selection mark, the images are not shown.

Applicable Items

View > Show/Hide > Image applies to images inserted into the body, Master Page, and page Background. The Show/Hide Image setting does not affect images used to fill object faces with.

Printing Images

Images are shown in printouts regardless of the Show/Hide Image setting.
