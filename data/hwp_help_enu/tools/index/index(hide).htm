Hide from TOC
 

References > Table of Contents > Hide from TOC

You can use Hide from TOC to hide from the table of contents specific paragraphs that have been marked as TOC elements.

Steps

  1. Click References > Table of Contents > Mark as TOC to mark the headers and then move to a paragraph you wish to hide from the table of contents.
  2. Click References > Table of Contents > Hide from TOC.
  3. The Hidden TOC code is inserted at the beginning of the paragraph. Click View > Show/Hide - Control Codes to see the Hidden TOC code.
  4. When you click References > Table of Contents > Table of Contents to create a table of contents, paragraphs with the Hidden TOC code are excluded from the table of contents.

Note

Hidden TOC Code Location

Regardless of where in the paragraph the cursor is placed, clicking Hide from TOC inserts the Hidden TOC code at the beginning of the paragraph.
