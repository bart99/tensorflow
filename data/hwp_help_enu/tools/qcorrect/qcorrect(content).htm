AutoCorrect List
 

Review > AutoCorrect > AutoCorrect List

AutoCorrect is a feature that automatically corrects spelling and expression mistakes as you make them while working on a document.

Steps

  1. Click Review > AutoCorrect > AutoCorrect List.
  2. In the AutoCorrect List dialog box, select the AutoCorrect List tab.
  3. Click Add AutoCorrect, type tommorow in the Incorrect Word field, and type tomorrow, the correct spelling of the word in the Correct Word field.
  4. Click Add.
  5. With Review > AutoCorrect > Enable AutoCorrect selected, typing tommorow in the body is now automatically corrected to tomorrow.
