AutoCorrect-Sound Effects
 

Review > AutoCorrect > AutoCorrect-Sound Effects

You can specify whether to use sound effects to alert you of AutoCorrect actions.

Steps

  1. Select Review > AutoCorrect > AutoCorrect-Sound Effects.
  2. A selection mark in front of the AutoCorrect-Sound Effects option indicates that sound has been enabled.
  3. To disable the sound, select Review > AutoCorrect > AutoCorrect-Sound Effects once more and remove the selection mark.
