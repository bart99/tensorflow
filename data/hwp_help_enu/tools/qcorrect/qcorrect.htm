AutoCorrect
 

Review > AutoCorrect

AutoCorrect is a Hwp feature that automatically corrects spelling and spacing mistakes made by the user. In addition, you can add custom words to AutoCorrect List and remove those you do not need.

Steps

  1. Click Review > AutoCorrect > Enable AutoCorrect and then verify the selection mark in front of the Enable AutoCorrect option.
  2. Type i (lower case) in the edit screen and press the SPACEBAR once.
  3. The lowercase i is corrected to the uppercase I.

Note

AutoCorrect Activation Point

AutoCorrect is triggered when an incorrect text string is entered and then SPACE, TAB, or ENTER is pressed to complete the word entry.
