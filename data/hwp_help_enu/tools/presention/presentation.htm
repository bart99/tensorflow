Presentation
 

View > Presentation

The presentation feature displays Hwp documents on top of a picture or gradation effect background for use as a presentation.

You can specify the presentation background type, transition effects, transition sounds, and application range by clicking View > Presentation > Presentation Settings. Once you have specified the settings, click View > Presentation > Run Presentation to begin the presentation.
