Presentation Settings
 

View > Presentation > Presentation Settings

You can specify the presentation background, transition effects, transition sounds, and the application range to create a diverse range of presentation styles.

Steps

  1. Click View > Presentation > Presentation Settings.
  2. Select the desired background type under the Background tab, transition, and transition sound effects under the Transition tab in the Presentation Settings dialog box.
  3. Click OK to finish configuring presentation settings.

Note

Saving Presentation Settings to Current Document

After a presentation, the presentation settings designated by the user are saved along with the document. Therefore, you can avoid having to specify the settings each time you run a presentation on a particular document by specifying the most suitable background for the document in advance.
