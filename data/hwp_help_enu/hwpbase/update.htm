Hancom Update
 

If Hwp gets modified or is updated with advanced functions, you can update your Hwp to its latest version easily by using the Internet.

Steps

  1. Connect to the Internet.
  2. Click Start on the Windows taskbar.
  3. Click All Programs > Hancom > Hancom Update.
  4. In the Hancom Update dialog box, update the program according to the guide.

Note

Customer Registration

When the program gets updated, Hancom Inc. sends an email notification to users who have purchased Hwp and are registered as customers.

To verify your version

If you click the Help icon > About Thinkfree Office NEO Hwp, the About Thinkfree Office NEO Hwp dialog box appears. You can verify your version of Hwp in the Product Info tab of the dialog box.

Check your Hwp version to see if your Hwp is the latest version.
