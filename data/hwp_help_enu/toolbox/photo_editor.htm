Edit with Photo
 

 

Photo allows making clear, vivid, and gorgeous pictures with an easy and simple adjustment.

Using Photo, you can make pictures transparent partially. If you apply transparent effect to the picture that is not in the PNG format, the file with transparent effect will be saved in the PNG format.

Steps

To sharpen picture

  1. Insert a picture into the document.
  2. Select the inserted picture and click the Edit with Photo icon in the Picture Tools tab to bring the Photo program.
  3. Click the Sharpen menu or the expand icon on the right pane under Easy Adjustment of the Photo window.
  4. Adjust the sharpness level of the control.
    The adjustment level is a total of 5 levels, and Step 3 is set by default.
  5. Click the Apply button to apply the effect to the original picture of the document and to close the Photo program.

To compensate for the red flash exposure

  1. Insert a picture that need to be compensated and click the Edit with Photo icon in the Picture Tools tab to bring the Photo program.
  2. Click the Reduce Red (Flash) menu or the expand icon on the right pane under Easy Adjustment of the Photo program.
    You can compensate yellow or red flash depending on the flash exposure in the picture.
  3. Adjust the sharpness level of the control.
    The adjustment level is a total of 5 levels, and Step 3 is set by default.
  4. Click the Apply button to apply the effect to the original picture of the document and to close the Photo program.

To apply transparent effect

  1. Insert a picture that need to be compensated and click the Edit with Photo icon in the Picture Tools tab.
  2. Click the Transparent Effect tab on the right pane of the Photo program.
    The task pane changes from Easy Adjustment to Transparent Effect and the Transparent Effect tab is enabled.
  3. Select the smoothness level of the edges under the Smooth Edges control.
  4. Specify the color spectrum between 0 and 255 under the Similar Color control.
  5. With the Transparent pointer, click the area where you want to remove color.
  6. Click the Apply button to apply the effect to the original picture of the document and to close the Photo program.

To change the view layout

  1. Insert a picture that need to be compensated and click the Edit with Photo icon in the Picture Tools tab.
  2. Click the Horizontal Split icon to place the Before area on the upper side and the After area on the lower side.
    By default, the Before area on the left side and the After area on the right side are placed.
  3. Apply effects to the picture and compare the original and the picture with effects.
  4. Click the Apply button to apply the effect to the original picture of the document and to close the Photo program.

  You can change the view layout by clicking the Horizontal Split icon or the Vertical Split icon to compare before and after. If you click the Single View icon, the After area appears on the full of the screen.

Adjust the picture view size and move the picture

  1. Click the Zoom Out or Zoom In icon several times to adjust the view size.
    Or adjust the slide bar on the bottom of the window to zoom in or out the picture.
  2. Click and hold the picture on the Before or After area and move to the direction where you want to see the part of the picture.
    You cannot move the picture on the Transparent Effect task pane.

More Information

Easy Adjustment

Bright

Make pictures brighter. The adjustment level is a total of 5 levels and No Effect removes the effect.

Dark

Make pictures darker. The adjustment level is a total of 5 levels and No Effect removes the effect.

Sharpen

Enhance the definition of edges to add clarity to pictures. The adjustment level is a total of 5 levels and No Effect removes the effect.

Vivid

Vivify pictures with colorful feeling. The adjustment level is a total of 5 levels and No Effect removes the effect.

Reduce Yellow (Flash)

Reduce the yellow flashed to balance light in the picture. The adjustment level is a total of 5 levels and No Effect removes the effect.

Reduce Red (Flash)

Reduce the red flashed to balance light in the picture. The adjustment level is a total of 5 levels and No Effect removes the effect.

Slim

Reduce the width of the picture to make a person in the picture look slim. The adjustment level is a total of 5 levels and No Effect removes the effect.

Transparent Effect

Contiguous Area Only

Make pixels contiguous to the selection transparent. Leave Contiguous Area Only unselected to remove all similar pixels from the image, otherwise the pixels contiguous to the selection only are transparent.

Smooth Edges

Adjust the smoothness of the edges where transparent effects are applied. The adjustment level is a total of 7 levels.

Similar Color

Specify the color spectrum between 0 and 255 for transparent effects. The smaller the value is, the stricter the color is analyzed to be removed from the picture.

Reset Button

Remove all the effects from the picture to reset it.

Apply Button

Close the Photo program and apply effects to the picture of the document.

Close Button

Close the Photo program.
If there are changes by using effects, the dialog asking whether to apply changes to the picture of the document or not appears.

Icon Description

Icon Name Description
Single View Display only one view for the After area.
Horizontal Split Display the Before area on the upper side and the After area on the lower side.
Vertical Split Display the Before area on the left side and the After area on the right side.
Original Size Display the picture in the actual size on the Before and After areas.
Fit to Window Adjust the picture size to fit to the window width and display the picture on the Before and After areas.
Zoom Out Zoom out of the picture by 1 level. The adjustment level is a total of 7 levels.
Zoom In Zoom in the picture by 1 level. The adjustment level is a total of 7 levels.

Note

Restrictions

1bit binary, 8bit color like scan image, and CMYK (32bit) are not supported by Photo.
