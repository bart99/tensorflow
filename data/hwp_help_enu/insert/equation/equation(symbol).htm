Entering Equation Symbols
 

Insert > Equation

When you use the Equation toolbox of the Equation Editor dialog box, you can write complicated equation symbols.

Note

  1. Click the Greek Uppercase icon on the far left in the second line of the Equation toolbox.
  2. Select a symbol you want from a list of all available Greek uppercase characters.
  3. The symbol you selected will be inserted at the pointer in the equation edit window of the Equation Editor dialog box.
  4. After finishing equation editing, click the Insert icon in the Equation toolbox.
  5. The Equation toolbox will close and the equation symbol will be inserted at the pointer in the body text.

More Information

Symbol Type

Equation symbols in the Equation toolbox consist of Greek Uppercase, Greek Lowercase, Greek Symbol, Sum/Set Symbols, Operational/Logical Symbols, Arrows, and Other Symbols. If you click the arrow on the right of each equation symbol icon, you can check a list of all symbols.
