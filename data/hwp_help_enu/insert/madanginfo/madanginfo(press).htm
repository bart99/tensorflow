Field: Click Here
 

Insert > Quick Parts > Field > Click Here

You can enter a guide instructing input in empty areas when the document template is opened and a short comment about the guide.

Steps

  1. Click Insert > Quick Parts > Field.
  2. In the Field dialog box, select the Click Here tab.
  3. Tip of the Content to Enter is shown with the default message: "Click here and enter content".
  4. Click Insert, and "Click here and enter content." appears in the edit window as red and italicized text.

More Information

Click Here Tab of the Field Dialog Box

Tip of the Content to Enter

Information in the box is shown as red and italicized text in the window.

Clicking on the guide shown in the window clears the guide and displays a Click Here field that is bound by oriental quotation marks and into which text can be typed.

Comment Content

Enter information about the Click Here field or a comment about the information that needs to be typed in. Comment is shown on the status bar when the cursor is placed inside the Click Here field.

Field Name

You can type the name of the inserted Click Here field. Designate a name for the field for easier identification when using multiple fields. Field Name is shown on the status bar when the cursor is placed inside the Click Here field.

Note

Modify Click Here

To modify a Click Here field that has been inserted in the body, place the cursor in front of the field or inside of it and right click and select Modify Click Here.

Select Click Here Content

To select the content of a Click Here field, place the cursor in front of or inside the Click Here field and press F11. Pressing F11 again selects the first previous object (Click Here, Drawing Object, Picture, Table, Equation, and Wordshop) from the current cursor position.

If the selected object is the first object of the document, pressing F11 unselects the object.

Print Click Here Option

Specify whether to print the document's Click Here information or not.

To exclude Click Here information from being printed, click File > Print and uncheck the Click Here option under the Advanced tab. However, information entered inside Click Here fields by the user will be printed regardless of the Click Here setting.
