Enclose Characters
 

Home > Enclose Characters

You can take the benefit of the Enclose Characters feature to enclose characters in shapes.

More Information

Enclose Characters Dialog Box

Characters

Type in the numbers and/or letters of choice to enclose in a shape.

Enclose with Text

Characters typed in Characters are typed one over the other.

Enclose with Shape

Select a shape to enclose the characters in Characters with.

Button Name Description
Circle Enclose the characters with an inverted circle.
Inverted Circle Enclose the characters with an inverted circle.
Rectangle Enclose the characters with a rectangle.
Inverted Rectangle Enclose the characters with an inverted rectangle.
Other Chars Enclose the characters with other shapes.

Enclose Inside a Shape

Characters typed into Characters are typed one over the other.

Text Size Adjustment

Adjust character size between 50% and 150%.

When Text Size Adjustment is set to 100%, the characters are inserted in their original size.

When the value is smaller than 100%, the characters are inserted on a reduced scale. There are 5 reduction ratios available.

With the value is greater than 100%, the characters are inserted on an enlarged scale. There are 5 enlargement ratios available.

Note

Spaces cannot be used in Enclose Characters > Characters.
